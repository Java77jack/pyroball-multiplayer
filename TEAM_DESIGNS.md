## Archetype Definitions

### Aggressive Striker (Tall)
- Height: Tallest of the team
- Build: Muscular, powerful
- Stance: Forward-leaning, aggressive
- Specialty: High power shots, dominant presence
- Animation: Explosive movements, intense poses

### Balanced All-Rounder (Medium)
- Height: Medium
- Build: Athletic, proportional
- Stance: Confident, ready
- Specialty: Versatile play, good at everything
- Animation: Smooth, controlled movements

### Quick Agile (Compact)
- Height: Shortest of the team
- Build: Lean, nimble
- Stance: Ready to move, quick reflexes
- Specialty: Speed, agility, quick passes
- Animation: Rapid, dynamic movements
